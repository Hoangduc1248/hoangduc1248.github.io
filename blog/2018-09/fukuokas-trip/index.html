Fukuoka's trip
  • Sep 19, 2018
  • 3 minutes read

Brief: A first IEEE conference of mine. I will talk about that I think about Fukuoka.

First of all this conference is a must have step to apply for my new scholarship which is called Rotary-Yoneyama. A very nice scholarship not only on amount of money every month, It also good about to have a very success Japanese consultant. Suddenly, this time I want to learn more about tax, and economy and then it will be nice to have this scholarship.

Morning 18 September 2018, I take a bus to go to Niigata, a beautiful nearby city which have a domestic airport. Wow first time go to the Southern of Japan, a bit excited and I thought that: What is waiting for me in Fukuoka? A new journey? I always like to explore and learn a new thing. This is the life we should learn a new thing or just going to die right away.

After two hours in the sky, I arrive to Fukuoka. DO you know what is my first impressive? A big city compare to my living city and very overcrowded, many thing new, my eye feel so excited. As a sense of a animal, look at the Google map I found out that we have a castle. Go there now - my mind just yielding. You know what? I go straight to the castle, using a traveler sense and Google map to go there.

But the more I expected the more I disappointed. First of all, should we call this a castle? It’s the way smaller than Aizu castle and I think it was destroyed too hard. But there is a very interesting thing is that you can see so many cats in the castle and it is very close to people. And on the top of the castle we can see the whole Fukuoka city in some sense :D. Ah! I think because I’m a Vietnamese and then confuse some Chinese, they talk and ask my by Chinese. Wow even that they don’t know English or Japanese, how can they traveling to Japan. But you know what? This city is like China. A lot of Chinese, Chinese store, Chinese restaurant. All I heard is Chinese. After all I back to my hotel for check in and check my presentation for the last time. The thing I like the most in that hotel is that I can take a shower for a hour without worry about anything, just relax. Feel so good.

Next day, I go to Kyushuu University for check in. The very old University, everything is old, even the tree. The day after I make a presentation on my field and fast after that, I escape from the conference. Go to the canvas city, very impressive city but it is full of Chinese tourist.

I spend my whole day at this place. A lot of thing to do. And at the afternoon we have a water Music:

Most of the times I just go around to enjoy the new view. It is a kind of fresh for my eye and my mind. After all it is a very nice trip. So I learn a lot of things from that trip and I feel relaxed.
